About

My name is Jeff McCormick. I am a self taught Web Developer. I started coding in 2014 learning HTML and CSS at National American University through taking an online course. I developed a passion for it and started volunteering with an organization called Crossroads for Women. The mission of Crossroads for Women is to provide comprehensive, integrated services to empower women emerging from incarceration to achieve safe, healthy, and fulfilling lives in the community, for themselves and their children. There website was created using Wordpress. I also made my first portfolio page taking an online course through thinkful.com. I also helped my friend create a hair salon website through wix.com called Hairapy by Mindi. I like to stay up to date with modern technology. I also enjoy social media such as Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, and Instagram.
